Online Advisory

Understanding the needs of our clients who are passionate about interior design, we offer an online consulting service. Our goal is to provide you with the guidance and support you need for your design project. Whether you're debating between two finishes, looking for the best location for Grandma's wet bar, or looking for color and lighting advice, we're here to help.


Our advisory service is customized according to your requirements and is billed by the hour. If you want more information about how we can help you, do not hesitate to contact us by filling out the form below. We will be happy to turn your ideas into beautiful and functional spaces.
