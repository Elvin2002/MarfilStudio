For this project we had to develop the concept and the Look and feel for the Olympikus brand store. We had the challenge of developing the approach of the first model store to later be replicated in Trujillo and soon in 3 more stores. We considered that a sports store had to show Technology, innovation and freshness, we transferred those words to materials and equipment such as exposed cement, commercial televisions and shop windows.
